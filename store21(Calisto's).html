Calisto's Portuguese Restaurant

Description

We are currently revamping and will reopen on 20 October 2023.

Launched in 1992, Calisto’s Portuguese Restaurant was founded and developed by an innovative and successful duo who are passionate about Portuguese food and flavours. The hearty family brand that has grown tremendously since its humble beginnings 24 years ago and is a name that has become synonymous with Portuguese food lovers.

The brand has gained popularity for its diverse menu offering, from the best flamed grilled Portuguese chicken to the finest seafood and meat, with meals that are freshly prepared in the traditional Portuguese way using only the freshest, finest ingredients available. Its authentic character, vibrant and electric atmosphere fills these eateries with a delightful experience that will bring you back time and time again.
