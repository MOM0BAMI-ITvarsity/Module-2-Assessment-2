Dufy

Description

Dufy is a Turkish men’s fashion brand with stores all over Europe and parts of the world. The clothing company was named after the world-famous French Fauvist painter Raoul Dufy, who was known for his colorful and fashionable designs. A true man of style. The store offers a range of sophisticated fashion items from suits, shirts, to belts and shoes. There is something for every man at Dufy. Look and feel good with Dufy clothing. Find this unique upmarket Men’s Clothing store in Pine City Mall. Find us on the upper level next to WeFix.
