Forever New

Description

Forever New is a fashion clothing and accessories brand founded in Melbourne, Australia. Our brand celebrates the feminine beauty of women, a signature style flowing through every creative element. Collections are inspired by global trends in art, film, music, and theatre as well as haute couture runway shows in Paris, London, Milan, and New York. Forever New creative teams work collaboratively to create unique harmonious collections, presented beautifully in elegant store environments for the shopping pleasure of our valued customers. Find us on the upper level next to Bellagio Jewellers.
